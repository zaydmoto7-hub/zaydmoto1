Frequently asked questions

FAQ

Do I need a licence to ride your bikes?

The Veracrus Smart 49cc can be ridden without a motorcycle licence. For the electric models (Touring Pro, Neo) it depends on the power — message us on WhatsApp and we'll explain what applies to you.

What is the range of the electric bikes?

The Touring Pro 1978 reaches 150 km of range with its 72V/40Ah battery, and charges from a normal household socket. The Neo is ideal for daily city rides.

Do the bikes come with a warranty?

Yes — all our bikes are brand new, still wrapped, with legal papers and warranty. After-sales service and spare parts are handled directly at our Tangier store.

Do you deliver outside Tangier?

Yes, we deliver in Tangier, across the northern region and everywhere in Morocco. Ask for a delivery quote on WhatsApp.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, payment plans are possible. Message us on WhatsApp at +212 6 74 96 04 94 for details.
